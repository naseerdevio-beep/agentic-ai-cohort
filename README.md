## 🎯 Executive Summary
The **AI Frontier Cohort** is a high-octane, 3-month elite residency designed to bridge the gap between academic theory and the rigorous demands of AI/ML roles at world-class technology firms. ## 🧠 Foundational Pillars & Mastery
The curriculum is built on "First Principles" to ensure candidates master the shifting landscape of Industry 4.0:

* **Agentic Paradigms:** Mastering multi-agent orchestration and autonomous reasoning loops (Plan → Act → Observe → Refine).
* **Model Optimization & FinOps:** Strategically balancing Frontier LLMs (Gemini 3 Flash, GPT-4) with Small Language Models (SLMs like Phi-4, Mistral) for edge-compute and latency-sensitive enterprise apps.
* **Data Sovereignty & Local Inference:** Learning to deploy and fine-tune models on local hardware/private clouds to maintain enterprise security.
* **Multimodal World Models:** Processing and generating value from diverse data streams including text, video, and spatial imagery.

## 🏛 Program Structure: The Sprint Model
The program operates on a **bi-weekly sprint cadence**, mirroring the Agile workflows of top-tier tech firms.

* **Schedule:** Every Sunday | 8:00 AM – 10:00 AM CST.
* **Duration:** 3 Months (6 Sprints total).
* **The Sprint Cycle:**
    * **Kickoff:** Technical briefing on latest AI toolsets and client problem statements.
    * **Execution:** 14-day build period using AI-native IDEs (**Cursor/Antigravity**).
    * **The Finale:** Live "Shark Tank" style demo to the Review Board and Client Representatives.
    * **The Reward:** Immediate cash awards for accepted or highly-rated solutions.
