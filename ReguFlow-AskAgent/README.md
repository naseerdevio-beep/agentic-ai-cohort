# ReguFlow-AskAgent

## Overview
ReguFlow-AskAgent is an intelligent agent designed to provide immediate access to regulatory information and compliance data. This solution enables organizations to quickly retrieve and understand regulatory requirements without navigating complex documentation.

## Key Features
- Real-time regulatory database queries
- Natural language processing for compliance inquiries
- Instant response to regulatory questions
- Multi-jurisdiction support
- Audit trail for all queries and responses

## Use Cases
- Compliance officers querying specific regulations
- Legal teams seeking regulatory clarification
- Internal training on compliance matters
- Risk assessment and mitigation
